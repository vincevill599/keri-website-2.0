About Keri Delivery
Keri Delivery, Inc. was established as a company early 2020 and fully rolled out the Keri Delivery applications across all platforms on the month of June in the same year. Spearheaded by a group of individuals with the vision of making top-notch deliveries and logistics services available to most Filipinos, Keri's primary objective is to consider the satisfaction of both our Clients and Riders making sure all sides will have a form of benefit. Keri offers services such as Padala, Pabili, and Papila. We also offer Last Mile integrations and are currently serving multiple LGUs as their preferred logistics service provider for their document / license deliveries.

The Visionaries at Keri Delivery
Alvin Villegas President/CEO Alvin Villegas, Jr. is the President and CEO of our organization, bringing over two decades of leadership in Operations, Quality, and Business Process Excellence. He has served as General Manager for both Six Sigma BELT Guru, Inc. and Core Enabler Business Process Solutions, Inc., and previously held an executive role in a global tech company. Alvin graduated with a degree in Industrial Engineering from Adamson University in 1994 and pursued postgraduate studies at the University of the Philippines, Northeastern University, and Grenoble Ecole de Management in France. His strategic vision and operational expertise drive the company forward.
Vector Maliuanag VP Operations Vector Maliuanag serves as our Vice President for Operations, bringing 20 years of expertise in Operations, Productivity, and Human Resources Management. He is also a Director at Trascom Builders, Inc. and an Engagement Leader for Coach Business Simplification. A graduate of De La Salle University with a degree in Business Management, Vector furthered his leadership and strategic skills with an MBA from the Asian Institute of Management. His deep operational insight and people-centered approach make him a key driver of efficiency and performance across the organization.
Arnold Abaya VP Finance Arnold Abaya is our Vice President for Finance, Human Resources, and Administration, with over 20 years of leadership experience in the semiconductor industry. His expertise spans Data Analytics, Data Visualization, and Process Improvement. He also serves as an Operational Excellence Consultant for Six Sigma Belt Guru. Arnold earned his degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of the Philippines in 1994. His analytical mindset and strategic leadership play a vital role in aligning our internal operations with overall business goals..
Francis Flores VP Marketing Francis Flores is our Vice President for Marketing, blending strategic insight with hands-on experience in sales and quality management. He is also an Operational Excellence Consultant and Sales-Marketing Director for Six Sigma BELT Guru, Inc. With a strong background in global tech companies, Francis has led initiatives in Quality, Project Management, Marketing, and Sales. He holds a degree in Industrial Engineering from the University of Santo Tomas (1999) and a Master’s from the Ateneo Graduate School of Business. His well-rounded expertise helps elevate our brand and drive market engagement.
