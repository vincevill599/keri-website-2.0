About Keri Delivery
Keri Delivery, Inc. was established as a company early 2020 and fully rolled out the Keri Delivery applications across all platforms on the month of June in the same year. Spearheaded by a group of individuals with the vision of making top-notch deliveries and logistics services available to most Filipinos, Keri's primary objective is to consider the satisfaction of both our Clients and Riders making sure all sides will have a form of benefit. Keri offers services such as Padala, Pabili, and Papila. We also offer Last Mile integrations and are currently serving multiple LGUs as their preferred logistics service provider for their document / license deliveries.
